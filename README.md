FAQ
Q: Where are my notes stored?
A: On your device, inside the app's private storage. No one else can access them.
Q: What happens if I uninstall the app?
A: Everything is deleted. Notes, images, all of it. Gone. So maybe don't uninstall it.
Q: Is there a iOS version?
A: No. iPhones are too expensive and so is the Apple Developer account.
Q: Can I sync my notes across devices?
A: No, and that's a feature not a bug.
